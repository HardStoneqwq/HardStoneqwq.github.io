电子学基础理论

电流 Electric Current

Electric current is the total charge that passes through some cross-sectional area A per unit time.

  1. 平均电流(average current $I_{ave}$)：

  2. 瞬时电流(instantaneous current I)：

    the limit as ∆t → 0, so that the current is the instantaneous rate (变化率) at which charge passes through an area.

    $I=\lim\limits_{∆t\to0}\frac{∆Q}{∆t}=\frac{dQ}{dt}$

  3. 1 A = 1 C/s (coulombs per second ampere A)

  4. The charge on a single electron is given by：

    $Q_{electron}$ = (−e) = −1.602 × $10^{−19}$C

    The charge of a proton is:

    $Q_{proton}$ = (+ e) = +1.602 × $10^{−19}$C

    1. 导体中运动的是自由电子，正电荷不能自由移动。
    2. 电子流动的反方向规定为电流的方向。

    electrons(电子) are free to move, not positive charges(正电荷), which are fixed in the lattice network（晶格）of the conductor. (Note, however, there are media in which positive charge flow is possible, such as positive ion flow in liquids, gases, and plasmas.

电压 Voltage

  1. 形成电流条件:电压 voltage potential difference(电势差) potential(电位) (导体两端的电压提供一个推动导体中所有自由电子运动的电动力EMF)。

    A device that maintains a constant voltage across it terminals is called a direct current voltage source (or dc voltage source),like battery.

  2. 电压的产生:

    电池内发生的化学反应产生了自由电子->自由电子在电池的负极端(阳极材料)积累形成电子集中区(该区域存在电子之间的相互排斥力即“电压”)->电池两端接上负载后,电子从电池的负极端扩散到电路中->电池负极端的导体端部的电子量增加->导体内部电压增大->靠近那些注入电子的自由电子受到排斥力迅速向相反方向移动->挤压相邻电子，产生连环反应(瞬间脉冲)

  3. 电子平均移动速度是非常慢的。(In fact, the drift velocity (average net velocity of electrons toward the positive terminal) is usually fractions of a millimeter per second.)
